Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered and baked pasta dish that is absolutely delicious. If you disagree, I don't want to be your friend. Sorry.

Ingredients

  • Wide lasagna noodles
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Basil
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Put down some sauce in a baking dish.
  2. Put down a layer of noodles.
  3. Layer with more sauce, ricotta cheese, and Mozarella cheese.
  4. Repeat until it's almost at the top of the baking dish.
  5. Bake and freaking enjoy my dude.
